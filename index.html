Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the service cost?

The service costs ₹60 per delivery. Out of this, ₹40 goes to the person delivering the item and ₹20 is our service fee.

How quickly will I get my items?

Delivery times vary depending on availability of runners and their current location, but typically items are delivered within 1-2 hours of placing your request.

What if I can't find what the requester wants?

Contact the requester through the provided details and discuss alternatives. If no solution is found, you can cancel the pickup without penalty.

Can I use Market Connect if I'm not an MTech, PhD, or MSc student?

Absolutely! While we especially focus on helping research students, Market Connect is available to all students on campus.
